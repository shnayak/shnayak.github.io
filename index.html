About Multiverse Technologies

"Multiverse Technologies" is the culmination of over 20 years of dedicated experience in the heavy power electronics industry. Founded by a seasoned professional who previously served as a Sales Manager at Aplab Ltd., our firm stands as a reliable bridge between top-tier manufacturers and businesses seeking robust power solutions.

With an unparalleled network of Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) and a deep understanding of market needs, we specialize in sourcing and brokering high-quality products, including industrial power supplies (>5KVA), online ups, and a comprehensive range of batteries, along with other related power electronics products.

Our mission is to simplify your procurement process, offering tailored solutions, competitive pricing, and expert guidance to ensure you acquire the precise power electronics products for your specific applications. Leveraging long-standing relationships and industry insights, Multiverse Technologies is committed to delivering efficiency, value, and reliability to every client.
